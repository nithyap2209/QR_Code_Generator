Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how QR Dada uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website or use our platform.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you use a website. They help us remember your login, maintain your session, and improve your experience on the platform.

How We Use Cookies

QR Dada only uses first-party cookies for essential and basic performance purposes. These cookies help us:

Keep You Logged In

Maintain your login session securely across the platform.

Secure Account Sessions

Protect your account with secure session management.

Enable Basic Site Functionality

Ensure core platform features work properly.

Process Payments

Securely handle payments through Razorpay and Stripe.

Privacy First

We do not use third-party cookies for advertising, retargeting, or external tracking.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the site to work. These handle login, session security, and payment functionality. Without these, you won't be able to use QR Dada's core features.

Examples: Session tokens, authentication cookies, security cookies

Functional Cookies

Used to remember preferences (like language, theme) and improve user experience. These do not track or share data externally.

Examples: Theme preferences, language settings, dashboard layout

Cookie Control

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling essential cookies may affect how QR Dada functions, including login and QR creation.

Important Note

Disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to use some features of the QR Dada platform, such as logging in or saving settings.
