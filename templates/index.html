Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QR Dada.

What's the difference between static and dynamic QR codes?

Static QR codes have fixed content that cannot be changed after creation. Dynamic QR codes can be edited anytime without changing the QR code itself, and they also provide scan tracking and analytics.

Can I change my QR code after creating it?

Yes! QR Dada offers dynamic QR codes that allow you to update the destination URL or content without needing to reprint or redistribute the QR codeperfect for those who want to create qr code once and update it anytime.

What stats can I see for my QR codes?

Our analytics dashboard shows scans over time, geographic locations, devices used, and user engagement metrics to help you measure campaign performance.
