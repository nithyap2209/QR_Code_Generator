What is a Call QR Code?

A Call QR Code (also known as Phone QR Code) contains a phone number that, when scanned, automatically initiates a phone call. Users simply scan and tap to dial — no manual number entry required. This also works as a simple form of a QR Code for Call, making communication even faster.

Call QR codes streamline customer communication, reduce dialing errors, and make it incredibly easy for people to reach you. Perfect for businesses that rely on phone support and sales.

Pro Tip: Include country codes in phone numbers for international compatibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the call be initiated automatically?

No, for security reasons, the phone app opens with the number pre-filled. Users need to tap the call button to actually place the call.

Can I track how many people scanned to call?

You'll know when people call you. For detailed scan analytics (without requiring calls), use our dynamic QR codes with tracking.

Do call QR codes work on tablets without phone capability?

Yes! On devices without phone capability, it may open VoIP apps like Skype, FaceTime, or prompt to use calling apps.

Should I include country codes?

Yes, always include country codes (e.g., +1 for USA, +44 for UK) to ensure the QR code works for international scanners.
