What is an Event QR Code?

An Event QR Code (also known as Calendar QR Code) contains all event details including date, time, location, and description. When scanned, it automatically creates a calendar entry that users can save to their device.

Event QR codes eliminate manual data entry, reduce errors, and ensure attendees have accurate event information saved directly to their calendars for timely reminders.

Pro Tip: Include full address and online meeting links in the location field for maximum convenience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which calendar apps support event QR codes?

Event QR codes work with all major calendar apps including Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, and any app that supports iCal/vCalendar formats.

Can I include meeting links in event QR codes?

Yes! You can include Zoom, Google Meet, or Teams links in the event description or location field for easy access to virtual meetings.

Can I update event details after creating the QR code?

Static event QR codes cannot be updated. For editable events, use our dynamic QR codes which allow you to change event details anytime without reprinting.

Do event QR codes work for multi-day events?

Absolutely! You can set start and end dates/times for conferences, workshops, or any multi-day events.
