What is a WiFi QR Code?

A WiFi QR Code contains your network's SSID (name), password, and security type. When scanned, it automatically connects users to your WiFi network without manually entering credentials, powered by an advanced QR code generator for wifi setup.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do WiFi QR codes work on all phones?

Yes! WiFi QR codes work on iOS 11+, Android 10+, and most modern smartphones with built-in QR scanners.

Is it safe to share WiFi via QR code?

Yes, it's safer than writing passwords down or sharing them verbally. However, only share with trusted guests. For businesses, consider using a separate guest network.

What if I change my WiFi password?

Simply generate a new WiFi QR code with the updated password and replace the old one. The process takes just seconds.

Can I create QR codes for hidden networks?

Yes, our WiFi QR code generator supports both visible and hidden networks. Just enable the "Hidden Network" option when creating.
