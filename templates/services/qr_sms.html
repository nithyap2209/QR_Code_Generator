Frequently Asked Questions

Can users edit the message before sending?

Yes! The pre-filled message is editable. Users can modify, add to, or completely change the text before sending.

Will SMS QR codes work internationally?

Yes, but remember to include country codes in the phone number (e.g., +1 for USA) for international compatibility.

Are there character limits for SMS messages?

Standard SMS supports 160 characters. Messages longer than this may be split into multiple texts depending on the carrier.

Can I track who scanned and sent SMS?

You can track incoming messages to your number. For scan analytics, use our dynamic QR codes with tracking features.
