What is a Text QR Code?

A Text QR Code is a quick response code that contains plain text information, functioning similarly to a simple qr code for text when sharing written data. When scanned with a smartphone, it instantly displays the encoded text message without requiring any internet connection.

Text QR codes are one of the most versatile types of QR codes, allowing you to share up to several hundred characters of text information in a compact, scannable format.

Pro Tip: Keep your text concise for better scanability. Shorter text generates smaller, easier-to-scan QR codes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much text can I put in a QR code?

You can encode up to approximately 2,953 bytes (roughly 2,900 characters) in a QR code. However, for better scanability, we recommend keeping your text under 300 characters.

Can I edit the text after creating the QR code?

Static text QR codes cannot be edited once created. If you need to change the content, you'll need to generate a new QR code. For editable content, consider using our dynamic QR codes with a redirect URL.

Do text QR codes expire?

No! Static text QR codes never expire and will work indefinitely without requiring any subscription or server connection.

Can I track scans of text QR codes?

Static text QR codes don't support tracking. If you need analytics and scan tracking, upgrade to our dynamic QR code solution which includes detailed analytics.

What format should I download my text QR code in?

We offer multiple formats: PNG (best for digital use), SVG (best for print and scaling), and PDF (best for professional printing). Choose based on your use case.
