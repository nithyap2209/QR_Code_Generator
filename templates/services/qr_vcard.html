What is a vCard QR Code?

A vCard QR Code (Virtual Contact Card) contains all your contact information in a digital format. When scanned, it automatically creates a new contact entry with your name, phone numbers, email addresses, company details, website, and more.

vCard QR codes are the modern replacement for traditional business cards, allowing instant contact exchange at networking events, meetings, and conferences without manual typing or data entry errors.

Pro Tip: Include your photo, company logo, and social media links for a complete digital business card.

Frequently Asked Questions

What information can I include in a vCard QR code?

You can include name, job title, company, phone numbers (mobile, work, home), email addresses, physical address, website, social media profiles, and even a profile photo.

Do vCard QR codes work on all phones?

Yes! vCard QR codes are universally supported on iOS, Android, and most modern smartphones with built-in QR scanners.

Can I update my contact info after creating the QR code?

Static vCard QR codes cannot be edited once created. For updatable digital business cards, use our dynamic vCard QR codes which allow changes anytime.

How is this different from a regular business card?

vCard QR codes eliminate manual data entry, ensure accuracy, can include more information (like social links), are eco-friendly, and contacts are saved digitally for easy access.
