Privacy Policy

At QR Dada, operated by Fourth Dimension Media Solutions, we are committed to protecting your privacy and respecting the data you trust us with. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

Key Privacy Principles

  • We do not sell or rent your data to third parties
  • We collect only essential information to provide our services
  • You have full control over your data and can request deletion at any time
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information

Who We Are

QR Dada is a web-based platform that allows users to generate, customize, and manage QR codes. The platform is operated by Fourth Dimension Media Solutions and is available for users worldwide. By accessing or using QR Dada, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for QR Dada, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • Login credentials (email and password)
  • Billing and shipping information
  • Uploaded content (e.g., logos, images, text inside QR codes)

Usage Data & Analytics

When you use QR Dada, we collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device and session data
  • QR codes you create and their metadata
  • QR scan activity and performance metrics
  • Interactions with specific platform features

Payment Information

Payment information is processed securely through third-party providers such as Razorpay and Stripe. We do not store credit or debit card details directly on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your QR Dada account
  • Generate and customize QR codes
  • Track QR scan activity and analytics
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Send account-related updates or service communications
  • Process payments through secure third-party services
  • Improve and optimize the platform
  • Ensure system stability and security
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate certain features:

  • Analytics and Tracking: Google Analytics, Meta Pixel
  • Payment Processing: Stripe and Razorpay
  • Cloud Hosting: Secure cloud infrastructure providers
  • Email Services: For account communications and support

These providers have their own privacy policies that govern how your information is handled on their platforms. We do not sell or rent your data to any third parties.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

QR Dada uses cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze usage and performance
  • Provide secure authentication

You can manage your cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the platform.

Data Security

Industry-Standard Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with access controls
  • Encrypted password storage and tokenized sessions
  • Role-based access control for internal staff
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage strong passwords and responsible access practices.
